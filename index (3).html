The Experience

A short, graceful path from stranger to familiar person.

Mediated by a shared book and repeated time together. No swiping, no browsing, no public profiles.

01

Five-minute intake

Tell us how you make friends, what you read, when you're free, and what kind of conversations energize you. Thoughtful, not invasive.

02

Matched into a trio

Our matching system pairs you with two others in your city based on friendship style, reading taste, schedule, and neighborhood proximity.

03

A 15-minute intro call

A lightly structured, low-pressure video call. Just names, neighborhoods, and a few warm prompts. You decide afterward whether to continue.

04

A book assigned to your group

We assign a book suited to your group's shared taste, reading pace, and discussion style. Usually short, always discussable.

05

Two or three discussions

Over three weeks, your group meets to discuss. Virtual or in-person. We provide prompts. You bring yourselves.

06

Reflect and rematching

After each cycle, you reflect on what worked. We use that to improve future matches — or to keep you with the same people if you'd like.

What We Believe

Friendship is a trust
and repetition problem.

🔒

Trust before scale

We grow slowly and deliberately. A few good matches matter more than many indifferent ones.

📐

Structure before spontaneity

Structure reduces the awkwardness of meeting strangers. A shared book gives you something to talk about before you know each other.

🔁

Repeated exposure over one-off matching

Real friendships form through repetition, not chemistry at first sight. We build in multiple meetings.

📖

Shared object over forced chemistry

The book mediates. You don't need to be immediately fascinating to each other — you can be interesting together.

✦

Small groups over mass sociality

Trios by default. Enough variety to feel like a group, small enough for everyone to speak.

◻

Minimal UI, maximal clarity

Every screen answers three questions: What is happening? Why am I seeing this? What do I do next?

The Matching System

Matched on what actually matters.

We compute a weighted compatibility score across six dimensions. The weights are configurable — and always transparent to you.

Matching happens in weekly city batches. Every match proposal includes a human-readable rationale, not a score. You always know why you were grouped.

The default group is a trio. Enough people to have a real conversation. Small enough for everyone to matter.

User Journey

The full arc,
step by step.

—

Visit & sign up

You land here, read how it works, and create an account with your email and phone number.

—

Complete the intake form

Five sections, four to six minutes. Your answers go directly into the matching engine. No public profile is created.

—

Placed in the weekly batch

Each week, we run matching across all eligible members in New York. You receive a match notification with your group and rationale.

—

Schedule your 15-minute intro call

Three time options, based on your overlapping availability. You pick one. We send reminders. The call is short by design.

—

Post-call: decide to continue

You each privately answer: Did this feel good? Would you like to continue? If the group reaches the continuation threshold, we unlock the book cycle.

—

Read and discuss over three weeks

Two or three discussions, with chapters and prompts provided. Virtual or in person — your group decides. Attendance is tracked quietly, not publicly.

—

Reflect and rematching

After the cycle ends, you reflect privately. Your trust graph updates. You can stay with the same people, get rematched, or take a break.

Safety & Trust

Trust is built in layers,
not ratings.

Identity trust

Verified email and phone required. Optional enhanced verification available. Your exact details are never shown to other users.

Participation trust

We track attendance, response speed, and cycle completion to build a quiet, private trust profile used only for matching.

Relational trust

Mutual continuations, completed cycles, and successful matches build a soft trust signal. No star ratings. No public reviews.

No-show policy

One missed session gets a gentle warning. Repeated no-shows reduce eligibility. Consistent follow-through increases your matching priority.

Reporting & moderation

Discreet reporting during calls. Silent admin flags. A moderation queue reviewed by humans, not just algorithms.

Privacy by default

Neighborhood visible only to matched users. Email and phone always private. Exact address never collected. Reflections private unless you share them.
